Who We Are

A Tale of Two Cities

Started in a small alley in Tokyo, perfected in the heart of your city.

Little Lemon was founded on a simple principle: Respect the ingredients. Our founder, Chef Hikaru, believes that the role of a chef is not to overpower nature, but to elevate it.

Every morning, we import the freshest seafood directly from Toyosu Market. Using traditional Edomae techniques combined with modern culinary innovation, we craft dishes that are visually stunning and deeply flavorful.
